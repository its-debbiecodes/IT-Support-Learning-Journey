# Day 1 — What does an IT Support Technician actually do?

I would like to think of an IT Support Technician as someone who solves technical problems for users who are frustrated, confused, and need assistance with their computers, printers, laptops, and other devices.

## An IT Support Assistant needs these qualities:
- Problem-solving skills  
- Good communication  
- Patience  
- Basic technical skills  

## IT Support tools might include:
- Ticketing systems like Zendesk, Jira Service Management, or ServiceNow  
- Active Directory for managing passwords, user profiles, and teams in Microsoft  
- Microsoft 365 for Outlook, Teams, OneDrive, and more  
- Remote tools like TeamViewer for managing user systems remotely  

IT support is basically **40% tech and 60% soft skills**, as they are often the first point of contact for most technical issues 💻
